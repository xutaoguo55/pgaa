## Submission Risk

Main remaining desk-review risk is not formatting; it is scientific breadth. The added Norman multi-perturbation extension improves comparator coverage but still does not replace a genome-scale original-study DEG benchmark. The manuscript should be acceptable as a cautious computational methods paper if the editor accepts proof-of-principle benchmarking plus stress checks. It should not be submitted with claims of broad genome-scale validation, formal causal discovery, clinical biomarker discovery, or fully completed raw-data reproduction.
